## Overview

After joining a test cycle, you can submit bugs for review.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Join a test cycle">
    Go to your dashboard and accept an available test cycle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test the app or website">
    Follow the test case or explore for bugs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Report the bug">
    Click “Submit Bug”, fill in the bug details, attach required evidence (screenshots, logs).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit for review">
    Save and submit your bug for review by the project team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
