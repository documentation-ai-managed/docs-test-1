---
title: Cannot Invite New Testers
description: Fix invite errors for new testers.
---

## Symptoms

- Invite emails not sent
- Tester invite fails with error

## Causes

- Invalid email address
- Existing account conflict

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check email format">
    Ensure the tester's email is valid and correctly typed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check if tester already exists">
    Ask the tester to log in or reset password if they already have an account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    If the issue persists, email support@test.io for assistance.
  </Step>
</Steps>
