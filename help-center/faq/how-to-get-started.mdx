## Overview

To start, sign up for an account at `test.io`. After login, onboard your team and create your first test cycle.

## Steps

1. Visit `https://app.test.io/signup`
2. Fill in your details and verify your email
3. Log in and complete onboarding prompts
4. Set up your project and upload your app or website
5. Launch your first test cycle with crowdtesters

## Need Help?
Contact support if you have trouble during onboarding.
