## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have the following ready:

<Callout kind="info">
- A valid email address for account verification
- Basic project details like app URL, testing objectives, and target devices
- Access to your development or staging environment for testing links
</Callout>

Review the [introduction](/introduction) if you need an overview of Test IO's crowdtesting capabilities.

## Sign Up and Verify Your Account

Create your Test IO account in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com/signup` and click **Sign Up**.

    Fill in your details:
    - Full name
    - Company name
    - Email address
    - Password (at least 12 characters)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification email from Test IO.

    Click the verification link. If it doesn't arrive within 5 minutes, check your spam folder.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Profile" icon="user">
    Log in at `https://dashboard.example.com/login`.

    Update your profile with:
    - Team size
    - Industry
    - Preferred testing regions
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Enable two-factor authentication immediately after signup for added security.
</Callout>

## Onboarding Walkthrough

New users go through a guided onboarding to set up billing and preferences.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Standard Onboarding" icon="play-circle">
    Follow the interactive tour in the dashboard:

    1. Connect your billing method (credit card or invoice).
    2. Select testing scopes (functional, exploratory, usability).
    3. Choose default tester regions (global or specific like EU/US).

    This takes about 5 minutes.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Enterprise Skip" icon="shield">
    Enterprise users can skip via support ticket. Contact `support@example.com` with your contract ID.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Create Your First Test Cycle

Launch your initial test cycle to engage Test IO's 400k+ tester community.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Cycles" icon="list">
    From the dashboard sidebar, select **Test Cycles** > **New Cycle**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Cycle Details" icon="settings">
    Enter cycle parameters:

    <ParamField path="name" param-type="string" required="true">
      A descriptive name like `iOS App Release v2.1`.
    </ParamField>

    <ParamField path="app_url" param-type="string" required="true">
      Your app or web URL, e.g., `https://staging.yourapp.com`.
    </ParamField>

    <ParamField path="duration_hours" param-type="number" required="false">
      Test duration (default: 24 hours).
    </ParamField>

    Set severity levels and pass/fail criteria.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit Test Requirements and Devices" icon="smartphone">
    Specify requirements:

````json
{
  "requirements": [
    "Verify login on iOS 15+ Safari",
    "Test checkout flow on Android Chrome",
    "Check responsiveness on tablets"
  ],
  "devices": [
    "iPhone 14 (iOS 17)",
    "Samsung Galaxy S23 (Android 14)",
    "iPad Pro (iPadOS 17)"
  ]
}
````

    Review and click **Launch Cycle**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
